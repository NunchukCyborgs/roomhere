Frequently Asked Questions

We'll try to answer the basics here. If you're still stuck, shoot us an email at support@Roomhere.io and we will be glad to help

  1. So, what is Roomhere?

    Roomhere is a platform for renters and landlords to come together. We created Roomhere after struggling through the process of finding our first apartments. We wanted to make the process easy for everyone to list, find and rent the perfect space.

  2. I only see listings for Cape Girardeau, MO. How do I see properties in other cities?

    Roomhere is local to the Cape Girardeau area, and we are devoted to bringing the best experience to our home first before we expand. If you think we would be perfect for your town, give us a shoutout on Twitter or on Facebook.

  3. How can I rent a property on Roomhere?

    Once you find a space you love, you can send an email to the landlord listed on the property page and begin the rental process with them directly. We are actively working on a way to simplify this process at Roomhere, so keep a lookout for updates!

  4. I am a landlord in Cape Girardeau. How can I update information on my properties?

    Updating your properties on Roomhere is an easy two-step process.

    1. Create an Account by tapping the link in the top right corner
    2. Tap "Update Property" on the property you want to update.

  5. How much does it cost to advertise my properties on Roomhere?

    If you are a landlord in Cape Girardeau, MO, you're in luck! We will list your properties for free, no questions asked.

  6. I don't want free advertising for my properties. How do I remove them from the site?

    Send us an email at support@Roomhere.io and we can help you with that. But we will be very sad to see you go.
